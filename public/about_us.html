About Us

Introduction

Dashansh-the free food app, “Donate a Bread”, a concept initiated to not only feed the needy,but also to manage the food- wastage in across the globe.

Dashansh is a sanskrit word, which means the 10th Part.Through Dashansh we want everyone to step forward and donate 10th part of their meal.If any kind of edibles is being wasted in your homes or. restaurants,install the Dashansh app and Donate

Throwing away the food might seem easy for us, but feeding the same food to some hungry brings happiness. Simply install the Dashansh app, upload your food item in the Donate food section, for people who want to eat free food, or search Volunteers near you, who can fetch the edibles and serve any hungry stomach.

Through Dashansh app, you can create your restaurant, where you can serve your customers and at the end of the meals, food which are in edible state can be served either in your restaurant for free or can be voluntarily be delivered to needy.

Dashansh encourages equality, free food, are not only available for poor and needy, but for everyone who is hungry.

According to a report by food and agriculture department of UN, food wastage is 1.3 billion tons which costs 2.6 trillion dollars every year globally. And on the other side more than 7 million people die of hungry everyyear. Therefore, Dashansh has been brought into picture, with an aim of establishing a collection and donation centre in every city wherein people join and contribute towards feeding the hungry, saving the wastage of food, and evolving a chain of donating and feeding.

Instead of dumping food in your waste bin, “Donate a Bread” to a hungry soul and contribute towards humanity.

Vision

Dashansh aims at establishing

A centralised kitchen in every city

A centralized kitchen where all the activities will be taken care of, and will be managed by personnels who volunteer for supporting the cause.

A centralized place where all works of collection, distribution, packaging, processing of raw materials will be done.

Basically, the centralized kitchen will be a hub or warehouse for collection, packaging and distribution. All activities around various district, villages, areas will be monitored through this centralized kitchen.

It will be the food corner from where the food delivering and collecting team with there vehicle will collect food items for delivering to Distribution and collections points situated at every place.

Raw materials, ready to cook edibles will be brought in here for further processing and then again sent to the distribution points.

Distribution and Collection Points

Group of 4 to 5 members will team up to deliver food items and collect food items from donors. There will be separate number of teams for collection of food from donors and distributing at various places in the city at different times.

People will be able to collect free food from various distribution points at different time of the day/night.

People will be able to collect free food from various distribution points at different time of the day/night.

For example: Group A, B, C, D, E will be delivering free food at different times mainly during meal for times(breakfast/ lunch/dinner), at different places of the city like near stations, bus stands, slum, village areas, temples, etc.

Another group say Group F, G, H, I, J will be collecting food items from different parts of the city, generally after meals

Another group, Group K, L, M, N, O will be indulged in processing and packing food items in the warehouse.

Vehicle Delivery

Various teams with delivery vehicle will travel across cities to donate free food items, covering maximum areas of the city, and its outskirts

It will be channelised through the centralised kitchen of every city. It will be convenient to locate the delivering vehicle through The DASHANSH app for customers who wishes to have free food.

It will also help in delivering free food at your door. Search for free for free food around you, and choose home delivery option, the delivering vehicle will be at rescue.

Pick up System

Customers willing to donate free food will have the option to use pick up System. Through DASHANSH app, they can easily find volunteers who can fetch the free food and deliver it nearby.

Also, there will be option of the vehicle, the delivering vehicle can act as pickup vehicle as well. If there is a home delivery order for free food, the vehicle can fetch the edibles and deliver it.

Pick up system will be feasible for customers who wish to donate Food, as both volunteers and the delivering vehicle will have the facility of packaging materials. The customers donating will just have to give away and rest will be taken care by the team.

IDEA Desk

Hello friends!

In order to survive we need food. But as per tolls so many people across us are dying of hunger. Some feed on leftover throw on streets and waste garbage and some beg.

Belonging from a very small city Bhagalpur, Bihar I have witnessed how people in slums lead their lives. My house itself is backed by slum area. I have seen how people would gather around my house during any pooja, for that tasty prasad, during occasions and my mom would continuously give away edibles for hours.

Being a Chartered Accountancy student, I have stayed in places like Jaipur and Kolkata. And being a foodie and staying away from home, eating from stalls, restaurants, MacD, KFC's was every weekend job. Hungry children and beggars would snatch my edibles. Hunger and poverty are two major problems our world is facing today.

I would like to share an incident when I was 17 years old, it was my cousins engagement and was organised at my place, and after the party the other day during the

wee hours around 4 am, I woke up to se off my aunt. The sight I saw was painful. Group of 7 people including 3 children were feeding themselves on the thrown away food of the party. Almost 250 guests had arrived and calculatively 40 to 45 plates of food was wasted completely. Since then, on occasions of my birthdays, my parents birthday, my siblings and best friends birthday, I would visit temples and donate edibles like bread and biscuit and offerings like sweets and fruits.

Almost like all teenager I got indulged in studies, but years later my best friend Ashish, last year put forth this idea, and helped in making this mobile application. Besides being an IT Business Analyst he is a website and mobile application developer. He helped in developing and maintaining the app. Dashansh was actually a one night call, when we decided to carry this out. With further discussions we brought our vision out, of channelising a processed way of donating and feeding.

Requesting each one of you reading this, please Donate, instead of wasting and throwing edibles, it will actually save a soul. Donate money, and help us. Help humanity. Participate and form teams, feed people. Volunteer and help people who wish to donate but are unable to step outside or don't have time. Manage wastage of food and raw materials used for cooking. Start today, from your home itself. Help people live happy lives and not die starving. And spread the word.


Thankyou.

Pragati Sargam.
