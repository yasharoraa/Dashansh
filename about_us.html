but also to manage the food
wastage in across the globe.

which means the 10th Part.
Through Dashansh we want

donate 10th part of their meal.
If any kind of edibles is being
wasted in your homes or

app and Donate.

Dashansh app, upload your food

people who want to eat free food,

Vision
Dashansh aims at establishing
● A centralised kitchen in every city
A centralised kitchen where all the activities will be taken care of, and will
be managed by personnels who volunteer for supporting the cause.
A centralised place where all works of collection, distribution, packaging,
processing of raw materials will be done.
Basically, the centralised kitchen will be a hub or warehouse for collection,
packaging and distribution. All activities around various district, villages,
areas will be monitored through this centralised kitchen.
It will be the food corner from where the food delivering and collecting team
with there vehicle will collect food items for delivering to Distribution and
collections points situated at every place.
Raw materials, ready to cook edibles will be brought in here for further
processing and then again sent to the distribution points.
● Distribution and Collection Points
Group of 4 to 5 members will team up to deliver food items and collect food
items from donors. There will be separate number of teams for collection of
food from donors and distributing at various places in the city at different
times.
People will be able to collect free food from various distribution points at
different time of the day/night.
Also, at various collection points people will be able to donate fresh food
items, raw materials and ready to cook edibles.
For example: Group A, B, C, D, E will be delivering free food at different
times mainly during meal for times(breakfast/ lunch/dinner), at different
places of the city like near stations, bus stands, slum, village areas,
temples, etc.
Another group say Group F, G, H, I, J will be collecting food items from
different parts of the city, generally after meals.
Another group, Group K, L, M, N, O will be indulged in processing and
packing food items in the warehouse.
● Vehicle Delivery
Various teams with delivery vehicle will travel across cities to donate free
food items, covering maximum areas of the city, and its outskirts.
It will be channelised through the centralised kitchen of every city.
It will be convenient to locate the delivering vehicle through The
DASHANSH app for customers who wishes to have free food.
It will also help in delivering free food at your door. Search for free for free
food around you, and choose home delivery option, the delivering vehicle
will be at rescue.
● Pick up System
Customers willing to donate free food will have the option to use pick up
System. Through DASHANSH app, they can easily find volunteers who can
fetch the free food and deliver it nearby.
Also, there will be option of the vehicle, the delivering vehicle can act as
pickup vehicle as well. If there is a home delivery order for free food, the
vehicle can fetch the edibles and deliver it.
Pick up system will be feasible for customers who wish to donate Food, as
both volunteers and the delivering vehicle will have the facility of packaging
materials. The customers donating will just have to give away and rest will
be taken care by the team.
Motive
Dashansh, is not a concept to be limited to a certain region or area, but we
aim to reach every hungry stomach of every village, district, city, state and
country. Our purpose views a society where no stomach sleeps hungry,
and wastage of food is at its minimal.
There are thousands of poor and needy we find across streets who sleep
many night without food. Some even don't have a single meal the entire
day, while others are some who are starving since days.
Besides there are people across the world who are not beggars or poor,
but they just don't have money, may be they are unemployed in search of
jobs visiting offices or someone who has stepped out of house without any
money, or some who are indoors and don't feel like spending , because it's
month end.
Free food donated by you people comes to rescue for these people and
many other people who can easily avail free food.
There is an angel in every single living beast. Why not bring that angel out
and contribute towards food waste management. Throwing away food
seems easy, but DASHANSH is easier to install, locate and DONATE.
